Typography

The typography used for the site will consist of two typefaces:
"Slabo 27px" and "Lato". Below are samples and what elements they will be used for.

Slabo 27px: This font will be used for the heading elements of the site. It is more bold and easy to read.
Lato: This font will be used for blocks of text, like the paragraph elements.
Being a sans-serif typface, it will be easy to read, but contrasts well with the font above.
