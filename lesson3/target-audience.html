Target Audience

The target audience are residents of South East Idaho in a wide range of ages. This site will be targeted more toward adults, but can be used by children and teens as well who are wanting local information.

Personas

Persona: Local Landscaping Buisness Owner
Fictional Name: Joseph Baker
Demographics: 45 years old, married, four children, Bachelor's degree in Buisness Management
Attributes: Ambitious buisness manager, great leadership skills, community volunteer.
Environment: Uses computer and mobile device constantly, keeping his buisness running smoothly. uses the internet for research and communication every day.

Persona: Stay-at-home Mother
Fictional Name: Mary Patterson
Demographics: 30 years old, married, two young children
Attributes: Busy, on-the-go, hard-working, involved parent.
Environment: Uses mobile device often for reading, recipes, information, and communication. Doesn't use computer much, mainly mobile to take on the go.

Scenarios
