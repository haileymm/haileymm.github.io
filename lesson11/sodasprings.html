Fun at the Farmer's Market

by Jackie Johnson

Every other Saturday in the months of May through September, the Soda Springs Farmers Market takes place. This well attended farmerâ€™s market brings the community together and gives various members the opportunity to sell their high quality goods. Many appreciate the variety and care that goes into the production of the produce and the special hand made quality of other products offered for sale.

Some popular goods sold at the market are honey in many different flavors from the Garret Farm, and flavored handmade butters (sweet and savory) also from the Garret Farm. The cinnamon butter is the most popular sweet butter, the sundried tomato and basil is the most popular savory flavor. Fresh Whole Wheat bread sold at the Stoddard stand, as well as some fresh Zucchini squash. Sweetened condensed milk made with fresh milk from the Williamson stand, and homemade pancake syrup in many flavors including, blueberry, cinnamon, buttermilk, raspberry, and chocolate. Also, fresh Asparagus From the Thompson farm, as well as fresh eggs(in many colors). Some non food items are also sold, such as hand-spun wool yarn, tie-died Fish Haven t-shirts, handmade flower pots, and more!

The market also includes fun for the kids! Each week, kids can participate in a scavenger hunt to see if they can find all the items on the list. There is a drawing each week. Each scavenger hunt completed has a chance of being randomly selected and the winner gets a prize.

Come check out the Farmer's Market, every second and fourth Saturday of the month from 8 am to noon, May - September.
